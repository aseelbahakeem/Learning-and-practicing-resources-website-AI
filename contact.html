ℹ️ About

These challenges are designed to push your problem-solving skills with AI use cases . Each one is LLM-generated and refined to make sure it’s actually useful.

What You’ll Get:

  • New Use cases challenges every week (I’m aiming for weekly, but you know how it goes)
  • Quick refreshers to fill knowledge gaps
  • A space to experiment, learn, and improve

Want to Contribute?
If you have real-world AI use cases from companies, feel free to send them my way at aseel.info.contact@gmail.com. Or, if you’ve got a challenge idea, just create a PDF and send it over. Alternatively, you can fork the repository, make your changes, and submit a pull request

Contributions are always appreciated! I know you all have great ideas, and your input will make this website even better
